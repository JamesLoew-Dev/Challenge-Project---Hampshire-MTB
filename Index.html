Hampshire Mountain Biking Club

Pedal. Hampshire. Together.

Welcome to the Hampshire Mountain Biking Club! We’re a friendly, community-driven group of riders passionate about exploring the beautiful countryside of Hampshire.

Whether you’re a seasoned rider or just starting out, we offer rides for all skill levels, with a focus on fun, fitness, and friendship.

Join us as we hit the roads and trails, discover new routes, and share our love for MTB!

Hampshire MTB Club is proudly sponsored by Cannondale for 2025!
